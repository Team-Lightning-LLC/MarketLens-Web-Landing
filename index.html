MarketLens

Financial Research Platform

Beyond Retrieval

Every AI product on the market does the same thing. You type a question and it gives you a generic answer in too many paragraphs with little to no sourcing. Some do it faster, some cite it better, but all of them stop where the thinking begins and all of them bury what they produce in a conversation you’ll never find again.

MarketLens is an analytical engine decomposing your most important questions and turning them into cohesive, complete financial research. Each document integrates qualitative with quantitative data to deliver insight that holds up under scrutiny.

The current research process takes a skilled analyst six to eight hours, costs between five hundred and fifteen hundred dollars in labor and takes away precious time you could be spending building portfolios or sitting with clients.

MarketLens is the research process at your fingertips.

What gets delivered

Your research deserves more than a chat window. MarketLens gives you a research environment where every document has a home, every follow-up has a thread, and every insight has room to grow.

MarketLens produces multi-page, fully sourced analytical reports built to hold up under scrutiny. Each research document is named, filed into the appropriate workspace, and ready to be highlighted, chatted with, or followed up on.

Every report follows one of over forty analytical frameworks, or one you define yourself. MarketLens decomposes your question into its core dimensions, traces implications through multiple layers, models weighted scenarios, and audits its own work as it goes so nothing gets left on the table.

Why

Most financial advisors manage dozens of clients, each with a portfolio that exists in a market that doesn’t hold still. Sectors rotate, macro conditions shift, and holdings quietly become liabilities when competitive landscapes change faster than anyone can track.

The work that should happen — evaluating how shifting market dynamics affect key holdings, researching alternatives, stress-testing positions against current conditions — rarely does. Not because of negligence, but because thorough analysis takes hours that don’t exist. Ultimately, decisions get made on experience and instinct instead of evidence and rigor.

MarketLens closes the gap between what the work demands and what a day allows. Judgment is what clients pay you for, so rather than replace it with an AI chatbot, we aim to ensure that every complexity gets the research it deserves. In ten minutes you have the information required to make an informed decision instead of a deferred one.

The Architecture

MarketLens is built by people who’ve spent their lives studying how markets work to understand the underlying dynamics that generate change. It is designed to engage deeply with complex questions.

Every research platform on the market is built to cover what’s happening. Not one of them is built to answer what you’re actually asking: how your client’s holdings could respond to a shifting macro environment, whether two holdings in your portfolio provide true diversification, or where the real weaknesses are in your composition.

MarketLens doesn’t publish coverage. It runs the analysis you need, on the question you have, at the moment you need it.

Custom research is only ten minutes away.
